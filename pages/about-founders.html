Our Founders & Patron

The visionaries who built our chamber music family.

Nils Korner

Founder & Mastermind of Wollongong Music Camp

Born in Czechoslovakia, Nils settled in Sydney in 1939. He qualified as a diagnostic radiologist, practicing in Cremorne and Brookvale. Music was the great joy of his life. For over 20 years, he played violin in a group meeting fortnightly. He envisioned the Wollongong Weekends of Chamber Music, searching out appropriate locations to allow amateur musicians access to professional environments. He passed away in 2002.

Rachel Valler OAM

ACMS Patron & Celebrated Pianist

One of Australia's most distinguished pianists. Rachel has performed as a soloist with most major Australian symphony orchestras and toured international circuits under Musica Viva. An examiner for the AMEB for several decades, she was awarded the Order of Australia Medal (OAM) for her massive services to music education and community outreach.
