Kirribilli Concert Organiser Checklist

Running our concert series is a rewarding privilege. Below is the step-by-step checklist to coordinate a bi-monthly recital program.

1
Verify Financial Membership: Ensure all participating musicians are active, paid financial members of ACMS. Encourage non-financial or external players to pay dues via the register system prior to concert date.
2
Draft Program & Notes: Aim for approximately 60–70 minutes of total playing duration. Forward electronic program outlines to coordinator Felicia Mitchell at least 2 weeks prior, and print 40 paper program booklets to place at the entrance table.
3
Sound-Checks and Venue Open: The Gallery Room is unlocked at 2:00 PM. Rehearsals and sound-checks should wind down by 2:40 PM so guests can settle in for a prompt 3:00 PM start.
4
Welcome & Acknowledgment: Start the program by welcoming the audience and reading the traditional Acknowledgement of Country. Remind listeners of the delicious post-concert refreshments, and request their help stacking the gallery chairs against the walls at 4:30 PM.
